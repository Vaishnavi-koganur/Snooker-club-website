Membership

Snooker and Pool venues operate on a membership system. Memberships can be obtained by visiting either club, anytime during our opening hours. To register for a membership, customers are required to bring the following documents.

In order to become a member, customers must provide;

* Photographic ID (eg. Passport, Driving Licence)

* Proof of address (eg. utility bill)

* Membership fee is a one-off payment of £35 for life!

Members are free to visit club, there is no need for separate memberships for each club.

Members are allowed to bring up to a maximum of 5 guests at a time, each guest will be required to pay a guest fee of £5.
